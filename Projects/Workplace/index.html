UX / UI Design

Workplace. Make the city your office

Web Application for coworking places alternative

Visit site →

Problem

Workplace is a startup that offers freelancers or teleworkers places to work in cafes, restaurants and museums.

Users need a convenient and simple interface to view available places, information about them and the ability to apply discounts and promotional codes.

Solution

Mobile-first web application with a list of available places, detailed information about them. And also the page with the QR-code for free tea, coffee and special offers
