Privacy Policy

Sasha's Gym · English (U.S.) · Last updated: February 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Sasha's Gym ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Account information – email, display name, and profile details you provide.
  • Workout and fitness data – exercises, sets, reps, sessions, and goals you log in the app.
  • Device information – device type, OS version, and app version for stability and support.
  • Photos – only if you choose to set a profile image (with your permission).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize the app (workouts, history, goals).
  • Store your data securely and sync it across your devices.
  • Improve the app and fix issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations when required.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Convex and related infrastructure. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information. Sensitive data may be stored in secure storage on your device where applicable.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With service providers that help us run the app (e.g., hosting, analytics), under strict agreements.
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. For details and how to exercise these choices, see our User Privacy Choices page.

6. Children

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy in the app or at this URL and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at the support or contact option provided in the app or on our website.
