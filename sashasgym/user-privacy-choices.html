User Privacy Choices

Sasha's Gym · English (U.S.)

You can exercise control over your personal information in the following ways.

Access and view your data

Your workout history, exercises, and profile are available inside the app. Sign in and use the app to view and manage your data.

Correct your data

You can edit your profile, exercises, and sessions directly in the app. Update or delete individual entries as needed.

Delete your data or account

To request deletion of your account and associated data, use the in-app support or contact option, or email us at the address provided in the app or on our website. We will process your request in line with applicable law.

Photo and media permissions

You can revoke photo library access at any time in your device Settings under the app’s permissions. The app will stop accessing your photos; existing profile images may remain until you change or remove them in the app.

California and other regions

If you are a California resident or in a region with similar laws, you may have additional rights (e.g., to know, delete, correct, or opt out of “sale” or “sharing”). We do not sell personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us via the app or the contact details in our Privacy Policy.

Contact

For any privacy request or question, use the support or contact option in the app or on our website.
